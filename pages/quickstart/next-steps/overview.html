One of the challenges with using a somewhat realistic application for a walkthrough is that you get some realistic challenges that pop up during the course of building it. I want to call out some of these challenges now, which will be added as another section at a later date. ## Problem: Form has no validation Form submission is an important part of many applications, and the current `lore-hook-dialogs-bootstrap` isn't a very practical solution. It's useful as an idea, and to highlight what a hook *could* do, but it's practical use is very limited outside of simply applications. The next version of Lore, `v0.13`, will focus on providing a real solutions for forms and form validation, and will try to leverage that to make the dialogs hook for useful and provide build in validation. ## That's all for now That's all for now.
